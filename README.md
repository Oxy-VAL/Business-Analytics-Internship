# RetailIQ - Intelligent Retail Business Analytics Platform

> Business Analytics Internship Project | ELiteTech Intern

## Overview

RetailIQ is a unified Business Analytics platform developed as part of the ELiteTech Business Analytics Internship. The project integrates four analytical modules that solve common retail business problems using data analysis and machine learning.

Rather than treating each internship task as an independent notebook, RetailIQ combines them into a single decision-support platform that helps businesses make informed decisions related to product recommendations, pricing strategies, customer feedback, and inventory planning.

---

## Project Modules

### Module 1 - Market Basket Analysis
**Decision Support:** Product Recommendation Engine

- Discover products frequently purchased together
- Generate association rules using Apriori
- Recommend cross-selling opportunities
- Suggest shelf placement strategies

---

### Module 2 - Retail Price Estimation
**Decision Support:** Pricing Advisor

- Predict retail product prices
- Compare pricing trends
- Generate pricing recommendations
- Support pricing strategy decisions

---

### Module 3 - Customer Feedback Analysis
**Decision Support:** Customer Insight Engine

- Analyze customer sentiment
- Identify customer satisfaction trends
- Generate business recommendations
- Support customer experience improvements

---

### Module 4 - Avocado Price Prediction
**Decision Support:** Inventory Recommendation Engine

- Predict avocado prices
- Analyze pricing trends
- Generate inventory recommendations
- Support procurement planning
